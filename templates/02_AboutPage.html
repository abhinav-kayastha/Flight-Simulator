About

Welcome to the about page, this page explains the purpose behind this game and the requirements to complete the game.

Purpose behind the Fly Green

The purpose of the game is to promote environmentally responsible behaviour and show the importance of being sustainable. In the game you will be given an “efficiency score” by being more efficient with fuel usage you can lower your “efficiency score” and once the games end the lower your “efficiency score” the better it is. We are also hoping that after playing this, you consider some sort of carbon usage efficiency in their own live.

Modes

The game has 2 modes:

1. Free-Roam mode

In the Free-Roam mode, your task is to transport passengers to unlock new regions. Each region has a minor goal that you need to fulfil to unlock a new region, the minor goal is to transport a predetermined number of passengers. You will continue to complete these minor goals until you achieve the final goal which is to unlock the entire world. Once the final goal has been achieved, you will be given your total “efficiency score” based on their performance as well as a few other details about your playthrough of the game. After achieving the final goal, you can either continue to explore the entire world or, restart and replay the mode to achieve a better “efficiency score” or move on to play the "Challenge” mode. We recommend you start with this mode to get familiar with the game since there are no restrictions on carbon emissions for the player, unlike the “Challenge” mode.

2. Challenge mode

In the “Challenge” mode, you will have a maximum amount of CO2 that you can emit, and this amount will be called the CO2 budget. If you go over the CO2 budget, the game will end. Similar to the “Free-Roam” mode, you have the same minor and major goals. However, unlike the “Free-Roam” mode once you fulfil a minor goal and unlock a new region, the CO2 in your CO2 budget will be increased by a specific amount. Once the entire map is unlocked, you will be given your total “efficiency score” based on their performance as well as a few other details about your play-through of the game. After achieving the final goal, you can either restart and replay the mode to achieve a better “efficiency score” or to go back to practice in the "Free-Roam” mode.

And with all these things in mind, we hope you enjoy our game and learn to be a little bit more sustainable.
